Introduction

This is The Absolute Idleon Guide, helping all the players navigate the game effectively in all stages of the game. Idleon Has 2 Main Stages: The Pushing Stage; which is the focus of all early game guides getting you to w6 as fast as possible, and the Fixing Stage; getting back to the mechanics you have ignored, or payed little to no attention to while pushing to w6. In this guide I will go over all of the mechanics in the game with as much detail as possible, for you to enjoy and play the game to its fullest.
