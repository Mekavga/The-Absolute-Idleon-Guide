Classes

Idleon has multiple class branches. Here’s a quick overview:

Warrior

High HP, strong early game, great for AFK fighting.

Archer

High damage, great for AFK farming and bossing.

Mage

Utility powerhouse, best for skilling and alchemy.

Progression

  • Unlock your second character ASAP
  • Do daily tasks for gems
  • Upgrade stamps and alchemy early
  • Focus on AFK gains over active play

Cards

Cards give permanent bonuses. Prioritize:

  • Damage cards
  • HP cards
  • Skill efficiency cards

Stones

Stones provide powerful bonuses for late‑game progression.
